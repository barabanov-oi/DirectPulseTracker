Template Guide
What is a Report Template?

A report template defines the structure and content of your Yandex Direct reports. It specifies:

  • Which metrics to include in the report (e.g., Impressions, Clicks, Cost)
  • The date range for data collection (e.g., Last 7 Days, Yesterday)
  • Custom naming and descriptions for organization

Once created, templates can be used to generate reports on demand or used with schedules and conditions for automated reporting.

Available Metrics
  • Impressions
  • Clicks
  • Cost
  • CTR
  • Conversions
  • Conversion Rate
  • Cost per Conversion
