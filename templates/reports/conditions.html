About Conditions
What are Report Conditions?

Conditions allow you to receive reports only when specific metrics meet your defined criteria, such as:

  • When cost exceeds a certain amount
  • When CTR drops below a threshold
  • When conversions reach a target number
  • When multiple criteria are met simultaneously

The system checks your conditions at the specified interval and generates reports only when triggered.

How Conditions Work

Each condition has:

  • Rules: Metric comparisons (e.g., "Cost > 10000")
  • Logic: How rules combine (AND/OR)
  • Check Interval: How often to evaluate
  • Template: What data to include in reports

When a condition is triggered, a report is generated and sent to your Telegram automatically.

Tip: Use conditions for alerts about important changes in your campaigns, while using schedules for regular reporting.
