Cron Expression Help
Cron Expression Format

A cron expression consists of 5 fields separated by spaces:

minute hour day-of-month month day-of-week
Field Values Special Characters Example
Minute 0-59 * , - 0 = top of the hour
Hour 0-23 * , - 9 = 9 AM, 13 = 1 PM
Day of Month 1-31 * , - 15 = 15th day
Month 1-12 or JAN-DEC * , - 3 = March
Day of Week 0-6 or SUN-SAT * , - 1 = Monday, 0 = Sunday
Common Examples
Expression Meaning
0 9 * * 1-5 Every weekday at 9:00 AM
0 10 * * 1 Every Monday at 10:00 AM
0 0 1 * * At midnight on the 1st of every month
0 12 * * * Every day at 12:00 PM (noon)
0 */4 * * * Every 4 hours (midnight, 4 AM, 8 AM, etc.)
0 9-17 * * 1-5 Every hour from 9 AM to 5 PM on weekdays
