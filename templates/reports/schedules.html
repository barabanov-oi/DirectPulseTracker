About Schedules
What are Report Schedules?

Report schedules allow you to automate the generation and delivery of Yandex Direct reports based on a specific time schedule.

When a schedule is triggered:

  1. DirectPulse generates a report using your selected template
  2. The report is saved in your account
  3. A notification is sent to your connected Telegram chat

Schedules run according to your configured timezone and will continue to run until deactivated.

Cron Expression Guide

Schedules use cron expressions to define when they should run. A cron expression consists of 5 fields:

minute hour day-of-month month day-of-week
Example Description
0 9 * * 1-5 Every weekday at 9:00 AM
0 10 * * 1 Every Monday at 10:00 AM
0 0 1 * * At midnight on the 1st of every month
0 12 * * * Every day at 12:00 PM (noon)
